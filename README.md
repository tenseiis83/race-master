# 🏎️ APEX Racing - Open World

An immersive open-world racing game built with HTML5 Canvas and JavaScript.

## 🎮 Play Now
**[CLICK HERE TO PLAY](https://YOUR_USERNAME.github.io/apex-racing-game/racing_game.html)**

## Features
- 🚗 **10 Exotic Cars** - Bugatti, Ferrari, Tesla, Lamborghini, and more
- 🌍 **Open World** - Infinite terrain to explore and race
- 🤖 **12 AI Opponents** - Computer-controlled cars racing around you
- ⚡ **Boost System** - Turbo acceleration with limited energy
- 📡 **Radar System** - Track other cars in real-time
- 🎨 **Cyberpunk Aesthetic** - Neon cyan and dark theme

## 🎮 How to Play
1. **Select your car** - Choose from 10 exotic vehicles
2. **Drive around** - Explore the infinite open world
3. **Race AI cars** - Compete against computer players
4. **Use boost** - Press SPACE for turbo speed

## 🎮 Controls
- **ARROW KEYS** or **WASD** - Drive your car
- **SPACE** - Activate boost (regenerates when not in use)
- **TAB** - Go back to car selection screen

## 💡 Tips
- Each car has different stats (speed, acceleration, handling)
- Boost recharges when you're not using it
- Watch the radar to track other cars
- Drive smoothly for better control
